第七章：男女感情

感情中的遗憾与现实

生活就是这样，异性之间的巧合真的太少了。当我们准备好表达自己的想法时，对方要么没有准备好听，要么已经等得不想听了。都说人生没有彩排，每一天都是现场直播。我们又何尝不是这样呢？我们所理想的，当然了，一般结果十有八九都实现不了。失望的原因要么是怀有不该有的期待，要么就是痴心妄想。

有些事情我们错过了也许还可以重来，但大部分人和机会一旦错过，可能就是一生的遗憾。所以，我们还是应该珍惜身边的每一个人，不要留下遗憾！

这世界上没有不吵架的夫妻。的确有很多人都向往双方结婚时的浪漫，但我想说，浪漫是一时的，吵架才是一世。世界上几乎没有什么美好的事物可以持续很久。人会老，但故事不会老。虽说浪漫仅一时，但我们绝不能错过。

找对象的一大禁忌就是看脸。容貌可以掩饰，但性格不会装太久。在人身上，越是容易被掩盖的，我们就越不能作为评价的标准。性格、个性、以及三观、爱好等，基本上都是内心升华的精神物质，其余皆不重要。其次，以上的观察与了解最好是在没有建立关系的时候就开始，以防对方有所准备。

最好的感情？莫过于她做好了饭叫你吃，他刚发了工资叫你保管。人生就是这样，最伟大的同时也是最普通的；众人敬仰的却又是最平凡的。有人说：“不以结婚为目的的谈恋爱都是耍流氓”，而我想说的就是：“不以离婚为目的的吵架都是在秀恩爱！”

人生苦短，及时行乐。这话不知道别人说过多少了，只有当我们真正体会过后才会明白，别人为什么会这么说。陌生人终究还是陌生人，尤其是那些随时可以断开关系的陌生人。对于他们，我们只需要做到**“好言相听，恶言相抵”**就行了。我们没有必要为这些人浪费时间。

我们也要清楚：自己强大，外人依附；自己弱小，外人欺负。 这句话是我们漂泊在人生海洋中不至于迷失方向的指南针。记住，任何时候，陌生人的突然来访，或者是周围人的突然善言善语，我们都要谨慎对待。因为这些人，若不是我们身上有他们想得到的东西，他们是不会注意到我们的。相信得越深，受的伤害也越大。

当代女性与欲望的释放

当代女性的变化，我仅限于了解。毕竟我不能妄加揣测别人的想法，是受限制还是渴望自由。没有女性喜欢被道德束缚，反而她们更想要自由的环境。于是就出现了内心与外表的极大反差。每个人内心深处都藏着一个没有受过控制的自己，也就是原本的自己。

无论是想展示自己的身材还是想吸引异性，这是基因所控制的。一部分女性想体验不同的快感。在物质充裕的年代，精神和肉体的双重刺激的确会让女性的欲望得到满足。不能说这件事情不对，这只是通过时间的演变，男女之间共同探索出来的结果，只是保守的大众一时难以接受，无法放在台面上而已。

古代有青楼，对于一些贫苦的家庭，可以把自己的孩子送到那里以维持生计。这里我就不得不说几句，历史总是惊人的相似。现在表面看起来确实没有青楼了，而且嫖娼也是违法的。但事实上，人们的乐趣不会减少，只会玩得更刺激、更花样百出。只要有钱，可以包养自己喜欢的，然后在自己的家中随意玩，释放男性的天性，为所欲为。一些奇奇怪怪的道具、玩法数不胜数。所以**“古代史也是现代史”**这句话一点没错。

感情中的努力与现实压力

有的时候是因为喜欢一个人，而对从未了解过的人镀了一层金身。可以说，目前的同学之中大部分人都是普通人，只是我自己想得太多了。但是人都会慢慢长大的，变得和别人一样冷漠。所以，之所以会后悔只是因为目光太短浅，看不到未来是什么样子。与其亡羊补牢，不如想办法让自己站在比以前更高的角度看自己，看自己做得是否正确。

年轻的时候总是害怕畏缩可不行，争取不来或者不敢争取，那就不要后悔，因为你没资格后悔。女人也是争取来的。

刚刚看了一些关于带男友回家见父母的视频，真的是让我大受震惊。是啊，谈恋爱的时候是不会考虑那么多的。可谈好之后，就要面临着父母、朋友、亲戚等各方面的压力。人各有异，无论是对哪一方都不会完全满意。父母讲究男方能力如何，是否有能力照顾好自己的女儿，家庭背景如何，如果门不当户不对，那基本上是成不了的。

双方谈恋爱倒没什么，只要自己喜欢就好。可一旦进一步发展，考虑到未来生活、工作、安家，甚至是生小孩，那么这将是非常困难的。之前的人们哪想得了这么远，压力也没有这么大，无论如何先结婚再说，先生个孩子再说。虽然这的确是一种不负责任的表现，而且基本上后面也很难日子过得好。

所以现在网络发达了，年轻人看到了，知道了，就开始为自己着想了。女方觉得不能随意嫁人，要各方面都比较好的。也只有那些家庭条件本身就比较好的才不会太在意条件。所以很现实的一点就是，如果你不努力让自己过上一个好生活，那么你就会被看不起，也只能找一个和自己差不多的女生结婚，甚至是没人愿意和你结婚，那将是痛苦的。在这一方面，别人只会袖手旁观，非常现实。

Chapter 7: Relationships Between Men and Women

The Reality of Relationships and The Value of Regret

Life is simply this: serendipitous moments between people of the opposite sex are rare. When we are ready to express our feelings, the other person is either not ready to listen or has waited so long that they no longer want to. It’s often said that life has no dress rehearsals—every day is live. And isn’t that true for us as well? The things we hope for and idealize will almost certainly fail to materialize nine out of ten times. The reason for disappointment is either holding onto expectations you shouldn’t have or simply engaging in wishful thinking.

While some things we miss can be relived, most people and opportunities, once missed, can become a lifelong regret. So, we should cherish every person around us to avoid leaving any regrets.

There are no couples in this world who don’t argue. Many people yearn for the romance of a wedding, but I want to say that romance is temporary, while arguing lasts a lifetime. There is almost nothing beautiful in this world that can last forever. People grow old, but stories don’t. Although romance is fleeting, we must not miss it.

A major pitfall when looking for a partner is judging by appearance. Looks can be disguised, but personality cannot be faked for long. The things that are easiest to hide should never be our primary criteria for judgment. Personality, character, values, and hobbies are all internal qualities; nothing else matters. Furthermore, it’s best to observe and understand these traits before a relationship is established to prevent someone from preparing a facade.

What is the best kind of relationship? It’s when she has prepared a meal for you and calls you to eat, or when he has just been paid and gives you the money to manage. Life is like this: the greatest things are also the most ordinary; what is admired by all is also the most mundane. Some say that “a relationship without the goal of marriage is just flirting.” What I want to say is that “an argument without the goal of divorce is just a show of affection!”

Life is short, so you should enjoy it while you can. Countless people have said this, but you only understand it after experiencing it yourself. Strangers will always be strangers, especially those whose connections you can cut off at any time. For them, we just need to “listen to kind words and deflect the wicked ones.” There is no need for us to waste time on these people.

We must also be clear: when you are strong, outsiders depend on you; when you are weak, outsiders bully you. This phrase is a compass to prevent us from getting lost in the ocean of life. Remember, at any time, a sudden visit from a stranger or an unexpected act of kindness from someone you know should be treated with caution. If they didn’t want something from us, they wouldn’t notice us. The deeper you believe, the greater the pain you will suffer.

The Modern Woman and The Release of Desire

I can only speak from what I understand about the changes in modern women. After all, I can’t arbitrarily speculate on others’ thoughts, whether they feel restricted or long for freedom. No woman likes to be bound by traditional morality; on the contrary, they want a more liberating environment. This leads to a huge contrast between their inner desires and their outer appearance. Every person has an uncontrolled, original self hidden deep inside.

Whether it’s to show off their body or attract the opposite sex, these desires are controlled by their genes. Some women want to experience different forms of pleasure. In an era of material abundance, a double dose of spiritual and physical stimulation can indeed satisfy a woman’s desires. This isn’t necessarily a bad thing; it’s just the result of a process that both men and women have explored over time. It’s something that a more conservative public finds difficult to accept and is not openly discussed.

In ancient times, there were brothels where impoverished families could send their children to make a living. Here, I have to say that history repeats itself in a surprisingly similar way. Today, on the surface, brothels no longer exist, and prostitution is illegal. In reality, people’s pursuit of pleasure has not decreased; it has only become more thrilling and varied. As long as someone has money, they can take on a lover and play however they want in the privacy of their own home, releasing their instincts and doing as they please with countless strange props and methods. So, “ancient history is also modern history” is a phrase that holds true.

Effort and The Pressures of Modern Relationships

Sometimes, because you like someone, you give a golden halo to a person you’ve never truly understood. I would say that most of my classmates are ordinary people; I’ve simply thought about it too much. But people will slowly grow up and become as indifferent as everyone else. So, the reason you regret something is because your vision is too short-sighted, and you can’t see what the future holds. Instead of “locking the barn door after the horse has bolted,” you should figure out how to stand at a higher perspective than your past self to see if what you are doing is correct.

You can’t be afraid and shrink back when you’re young. If you don’t fight for something or are afraid to fight for it, you have no right to regret it. A woman must also be fought for.

I just watched a few videos about boyfriends meeting their girlfriends’ parents, and I was shocked. It’s true that when you’re dating, you don’t think about these things. But after you get serious, you face pressure from your parents, friends, and relatives. Everyone is different, and neither side will be completely satisfied. Parents are concerned about the man’s ability to take care of their daughter and his family background. If the two families are not a good match, it will be very difficult for the relationship to work out.

Dating is fine as long as you like each other. But once you take it a step further and consider a future life, work, settling down, and even having children, it becomes incredibly difficult. People in the past didn’t think so far ahead, and the pressure wasn’t as great. They would get married first, and have a child first. While this is irresponsible, it’s also true that their lives were harder afterward. With the rise of the internet, young people have seen and learned this, so they are starting to look out for themselves. Women feel they can’t just marry anyone; they need someone who is well-rounded. Only those who come from well-off families don’t care too much about these conditions.

So, here’s a very realistic point: if you don’t work hard to live a good life, you will be looked down upon. You’ll be forced to marry someone on your level, or even worse, no one will be willing to marry you at all, which would be painful. In this regard, others will just stand by and watch. It’s very realistic.
