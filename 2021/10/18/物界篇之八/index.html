第六章：个人问题

寻找更好的环境

目前看来，我的家里并不能让我很好地进行思考。时间久了，我会变得和以前一样，一事无成。可能是我的家里太安逸了，我必须选择一个像学校那样的环境，有危机意识，能够沉下心学习，很少被环境影响。因此，接下来的暑假环境选择我要慎重考虑。由于价值观理念的不同，我必须逆向选择一个我认为能让我独立思考的环境，以达到我暑假学习和未来计算机学习的目的。

我觉得待在家里就是一个消磨意志的地方，即使再有远大志向，如果处于这样的环境，我也很难有所作为。所以我必须离开这里。

认清世界的本质

看得多了，成熟了，才慢慢发现这个社会的本质，国家的本质，甚至是世界的本质。至少我现在不想去改变什么，我也没有好的建议。这个地球本身就是一个遵循着一定规律但自由度极高的环境。我们的能力强大到可以改变大部分事物，改变环境，改变人类的发展方向。人类社会已经经历了很久，但真正的发展迅速也只是从近代的工业革命开始的。

我很难想象一千多年来，人们都认为“力是维持物体运动”的说法。一千年现在看来几乎没有人会等待，可在历史的长河中，一瞬间就过去了。现在的社会发展得可真快，我们想知道的、我们不知道的，全都知晓了。当然，我身处发展如此快的信息时代，仍然心存顾虑。我已经不是第一次提到我的顾虑了：人类的发展方向是好的，但我不知道人类是否会适应那时的发展。

就像我曾经说的，我不会绝对相信任何一句话、任何一个人。每个人都有可能成为我的怀疑对象，我只相信我自己和我的能力。不只是我，很多人都已经意识到在这个大数据环境下人们的处境。意志力差、自控能力差的人，都很容易被**“纵欲”**所害。这是一个精明的世界，因为有一群精明的人在操控着这个世界。或者更可怕的是，我们的三观和认知都是被他们计算好的，一切都在他们的掌握之中。

情感与个人目标的抉择

对于感情，怎么说呢？我是从懵懂中直接进入了成熟。之前我也说过，胡琪喻成了我唯一过不去的坎。即使我现在可以放下她了，她是我唯一的初恋。之后，我便看透了这人生的杂陈，不想再经历这方面的事情了。其实我在很多方面都有很好的想法和解决问题的办法，而我给自己设定的方针就是：不能动用感情，也不要动感情。因为我已经因为感情而做了太多错误的选择和事情，我不想再继续这么愚蠢下去了。

人生就是一场慢慢成熟，然后让自己变得强大，最后证明自己强大的过程。所以我认为，在我的上述目标还没有完成之前，所有的感情都将是我这条路上的绊脚石。人生嘛，就是在跌跌撞撞的路上成长过来的，经历过坎坷才是最好的。回到话题上，其实我现在对感情没有抱多大希望，甚至根本没有考虑过未来我的感情生活。到目前为止，我仍然长期处于独行的状态。要对象干嘛？结婚干嘛？什么在朋友中炫耀啦，受不了长辈催促啦，对我来说，这一切都不重要。我的电脑才是最重要的，我的目标就是成为一名技术黑客，保护网络安全。至于那些别人都认为是人生必不可少的事情，都留给未来吧。

无论遇到什么事情，都不要有什么消极的情绪。首先，那些情绪并不能解决问题；其次，它们没有任何好处。遇到事情，能解决的话就力挽狂澜地解决；不能解决的就做好最坏的打算，弄清楚这个世界运行的本质，弄清楚自己的处境，静观万物运行，让一切都在我的掌握之中。这才是我的目的。

所以我可以非常认真地说，在我们的人生还没有稳定之前，所有的放松都是对未来的不负责任。人生本来就是同龄人一起在奔跑，有时候谁超越了谁都是非常正常的事情。

固化的社会阶层

世界本来没有路，走的人多了也就成了路。世界原本有很多路，走着走着就发现，有一条装饰得非常宽敞的大路，可以非常方便地通行，但是前提是你得符合他们的要求，然后被他们划分成三六九等之后，再分批让你行走。有的人不能走，就会非常羡慕。

是时候该静下来好好想想我的未来了，比如我什么时候找女朋友啦，什么时候结婚啦之类的。这几天听到了别人所经历的故事，感觉和我之前所讲的差不多，唯一有瑕疵的就是我说的可能有点极端了，毕竟我经历的还很少，考虑得并不是很充分。

对于比较贫困的家庭来说，他们的孩子面对的压力是非常大的。他们不仅需要努力上一个好大学，同时又要为自己的生活和家庭问题而苦恼。若想要出人头地，就必须要沿着国家给你规划的路走。

同时，现在这个社会的阶层慢慢固化，想要跨越这个阶层是难上加难。不是有这样一个规律吗？越是有钱的人就越是容易挣到钱，因为他们有资本、有人脉、有背景之类的。并且，越是有钱人，他们也越是意识到教育的重要性，即他们用现有的条件让自己看得更远，为了让自己更好地发展，注重孩子的教育。虽说现在的国家相较于国外还是比较公平的，可是仔细观察后发现，所谓的公平完全就是骗人的。有钱人的孩子上的都是私立学校，领导干部的孩子也都在特殊的学校。他们的孩子从一开始起点就非常高，德智体美劳全面发展。所以对于我们平民来说，我们不应该安于现状。

这个世界没有我们做不到的，只有我们想不到的。把目标看得更长远些，不要把情绪放在现在这个环境中。又不是在这里生活一辈子，不用费心思在这里，做好自己的事情就行了。毕竟我们是带不来也带不走的，在人类择优计划中，我争取通过自己的努力，找一个和我性格相同的女生作伴。我似乎对**“对象”**又有了新的定义：无论我怎样孤独，到老总是需要有人照顾的，有人说话有人陪，这些也只有对象才能做到。不要因为贫穷而限制了自己的想象，要感谢贫穷把我们变得这么强大。世界上本没有成功，而仅仅是知识与财富积累的多少。知识和财富可以创造，但是我们却带不走。人类可以消失，但是精神却可以永远地流传下去。

浮躁与自控的考验

现在的大多数年轻人可能都会有这样一个问题，那就是能力和浮躁成正比。我认为这种现象是很不正常的。真正强大的人，往往不会随意表现自己。**“言多必失”**不知道有多少人说过，可又有太多的人栽在这上面。管好自己的这张嘴是非常困难的。生活中无处不在的微小行为都可能在挑战我们的能力与极限。

提升能力的过程中，就像是在往自己的桶里放各种物品。有些人放进一点点，再压一压，一直非常实在地进行着。也有些人，刚装进去一点东西，就把桶晃得想掉出两个物品。所以我们面对这些的态度，决定了我们未来的高度。

还有一点就是，我们在学习的过程中，应当需要一个可以管得住自己的人，在我们娱乐结束之后，能够快速静下心来投入到学习当中。

我突然想起自己的处境，有时候把自己封闭起来确实可以短暂地认清自己，提高自己。但如果一直把自己封闭起来，就会与这个环境脱离，看不清自己。每个人内心都有一个自认为的自己，这个自己会随着社会环境随时改变，从而调整到符合这个环境。但是如果一直封闭自己，那么就会产生很大差异。所以不能断开与环境乃至世界的联系，尤其是本来和自己水平相差无几的人。不要只凭你自己觉得、你自己认为。你自己认为仅仅是你自己认为。如果你认为的自己比别人眼中的自己更低调，那么还行；否则别人就会觉得你很自大。当然也不能完全顾及别人的看法，做一个参考就行。

身体本能与意志力的斗争

应该是很久以前我就发现的问题，那就是我们生活中所说的劳逸结合。对于我们身体的本职工作就是想劳动后获得回报，也就是先苦后甜。对于我来说，目前的状况的确是这样。如果今天我用了很长时间去学习，那么第二天我就很自然地忘掉我昨天的任务。甚至我的大脑也在欺骗我，那就是我完全想不起来我还有工作没有做完，直到我休息了很久之后，我才突然意识到我还有很多任务。

我现在也是非常清楚我这些养成的坏习惯是非常不好的，但是就是这种我们自然而然的习惯，往往是我们身体最想要的。而理智违背肉体，这是做出艰难决定的重要一步。按照意志力的说法中，往往理智打不过肉体，或者是理智没有控制权，也就是我的大脑中一直想着去工作，但我的身体就是不行动，一直拖到事情拖不下去的时候。

Chapter 6: Personal Issues

The Search for a Better Environment

My current home environment doesn’t allow me to think clearly. If I stay here for too long, I’ll become the same as before—nothing. Maybe my home is too comfortable. I need to find an environment more like a school, one that fosters a sense of urgency, where I can focus on learning and not be easily influenced by my surroundings. Therefore, I must carefully consider my choice of environment for the upcoming summer break. Due to my different values, I have to make a counterintuitive choice and select a place that enables independent thinking. This is crucial for me to achieve my summer learning goals and my future goals in computer science. I feel that staying at home will simply wear down my ambition. No matter how great my aspirations, it will be hard to achieve anything in this environment. I must leave.

The Nature of Reality and a World of My Own

After seeing and experiencing more, I’ve gradually come to understand the nature of society, the country, and even the world. At this point, I don’t want to change anything, as I don’t have a good solution yet. This Earth itself follows certain rules, but it has an extremely high degree of freedom. Our abilities are powerful enough to change most things—the environment, and even the direction of human development. Human society has existed for a long time, but its rapid development only began with the modern Industrial Revolution. I can’t imagine that for over a thousand years, people believed that force was required to maintain motion. A thousand years seems like an eternity now, but in the long river of history, it happened in an instant.

Society today is developing so fast. Everything we’ve wanted to know, and everything we didn’t know we wanted to know, is now known. Of course, even in this fast-paced information age, I remain cautious. I have expressed my concerns before: the direction of human development is good, but I don’t know if humanity can truly adapt. As I once said, I will never absolutely believe any single word or person. Everyone can be a subject of my suspicion. I only trust myself and my abilities. It’s not just me; many people have realized the human condition in this big data environment. Those with poor willpower and self-control can easily fall prey to their desires. This is a smart world, because a group of smart people are controlling it. Or what’s even scarier is that our values and perceptions have been calculated, and everything is under their control.

The Pursuit of Power Over Emotion

My views on relationships? How should I put it? I went from a state of ignorance directly into maturity. As I’ve said before, Hu Qiyu became my one and only hurdle, my first love, even if I can let her go now. After that, I saw through the complexities of life and no longer wanted to experience this kind of thing. In many areas, I have good ideas and solutions. But the rule I’ve set for myself is not to use or get emotional, because I’ve already made too many foolish choices and decisions because of it. I don’t want to continue being so stupid. Life is a process of gradually maturing, becoming stronger, and then proving your strength. Therefore, I believe that until I have completed my goals, all emotions will be stumbling blocks on my path.

Life is about growing through a bumpy journey, and experiencing hardships is the best way to do so. Now, back to the topic. I don’t have high hopes for relationships, and I haven’t even considered my future romantic life. To this day, and for a long time to come, I will be a loner. What’s the point of a partner? Or getting married? The need to show off to friends or the pressure from the older generation—none of this matters to me. My computer is what’s most important. My goal is to become a technical hacker and protect cyberspace. I’ll leave all those things others consider essential parts of life for the future.

No matter what I encounter, I won’t have negative emotions. First, because they don’t solve problems, and second, because they have no benefits. If I can solve a problem, I’ll do everything I can. If I can’t, I’ll prepare for the worst, understand the true nature of how the world operates, know my place in it, and calmly observe everything. My goal is to have everything under my control.

Therefore, I can very seriously say that before our lives are stable, any form of relaxation is a sign of irresponsibility toward the future. Life is a race where peers run together. It’s normal for someone to surpass another.

Pathways and The Rigidity of Society

The world originally had no roads; roads were created as more people walked them. However, the world also had many roads, and as people walked, they discovered a grand, well-paved road that was very convenient. But you must meet their requirements to be sorted into different classes before you’re allowed to travel on it. Those who aren’t allowed to are incredibly envious.

It’s time for me to calm down and think about my future, like when I’ll get a girlfriend or get married. In the past few days, I’ve listened to other people’s stories and feel that they are similar to what I’ve said before. The only flaw is that I might have been a bit extreme because I haven’t experienced enough and haven’t considered everything fully.

For children from poor families, the pressure they face is immense. They not only need to work hard to get into a good university, but they also have to worry about their lives and family issues. If they want to make it big, they must follow the path the country has planned for them. At the same time, the class system in our society is gradually becoming solidified, and it is extremely difficult to cross those boundaries. Isn’t there a rule that the richer you are, the easier it is to make money? This is because they have capital, connections, and backgrounds. Furthermore, the richer people are, the more they realize the importance of education. They use their current conditions to see farther and focus on their children’s education for better development.

Although the country is relatively fair compared to some others, upon closer inspection, this “fairness” is a lie. The children of the wealthy attend private schools, and the children of leaders are in special schools. Their starting points are very high, with a well-rounded education. So, as common people, we shouldn’t be content with the status quo. There’s nothing in this world we can’t do; there are only things we haven’t thought of. Look at your goals in the long term, and don’t let your emotions get caught up in your current environment. You’re not going to live here your whole life, so don’t waste your energy on it. Just do what you have to do.

After all, we brought nothing into this world, and we can’t take anything with us when we leave. In humanity’s plan for optimal selection, I’ll strive to find a girl with a similar personality to mine. I seem to have a new definition for a “partner.” No matter how lonely I am, I’ll need someone to care for me in old age—someone to talk to and be with. Only a partner can provide that. Don’t let poverty limit your imagination. We should be thankful for poverty because it has made us so strong. There is no such thing as success; there is only the accumulation of knowledge and wealth. Knowledge and wealth can be created, but we can’t take them with us. Humanity may disappear, but our spirit can live on forever.

The Dangers of Impatience and the Importance of Discipline

Most young people today have a problem where their ability and their impatience are directly proportional. I think this is very abnormal. Truly strong people don’t show off casually. Many people have said, “The more you talk, the more you risk saying the wrong thing,” yet so many fall into this trap. Controlling your mouth is incredibly difficult. The small behaviors in life constantly challenge our ability and limits.

The process of improving your abilities is like adding various items to a bucket. Some people add a little at a time and press it down, working in a very solid way. Others fill it with just a little and shake the bucket so much that they almost lose what’s inside. The attitude we have toward these things determines our future potential. Another point is that during the learning process, you need someone who can keep you in check, so that after you’re done with your leisure time, you can quickly calm down and get back to studying.

I’ve suddenly remembered my own situation. Sometimes isolating yourself can help you get a clear and quick understanding of yourself and improve. But if you’re always isolated, you’ll detach from your environment and lose sight of yourself. Everyone has a self-image that changes with their social environment, adjusting to fit in. But if you stay isolated, there will be a big difference between your self-image and reality. So you can’t cut off your connection to the environment and the world, especially with people who are on the same level as you. Don’t just rely on what you think you are. What you think is only what you think. If you think of yourself as more low-key than others see you, that’s fine. Otherwise, they’ll think you’re arrogant. Of course, you can’t care too much about what others think. Just use it as a reference.

The Struggle Between Logic and the Body

It’s a problem I noticed a long time ago. The “balance between work and rest” we talk about. Our body’s natural inclination is to work and then get a reward—first bitterness, then sweetness. For me, my current situation is exactly this. If I spend a lot of time studying one day, the next day I’ll naturally forget my tasks. My brain even deceives me, making me completely unable to remember that I have unfinished work until I’ve rested for a long time. Only then do I suddenly realize I have many tasks.

I am very aware that these bad habits are not good, but these natural habits are what my body wants most. Reason defying the body is an important step in making a difficult decision. According to the idea of willpower, reason often loses to the body, or reason has no control. My brain will keep thinking about work, but my body just won’t act until the very last moment.
