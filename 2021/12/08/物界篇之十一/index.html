后期素材整理

我觉得有时间去找一些关于人性的书籍，想要立足于社会，首先要知己知彼，我还有很多需要了解的地方。现在我已经非常在乎别人所正在经历的事情，时不时的为他们而感到惋惜，可最终我发现现在的我只能先顾好我自己。看着别人，我无能为力，所以首先我应该先自己努力，然后才能帮助别人。

在我现在的生活中，我不完全相信每一个人，只有我自己相信自己。打破常规，走不同寻常的路，跟别人不一样是我的宗旨。当然，异想天开任何人都可以做到，付出实际才是根本。我不喜欢所谓的心灵鸡汤，这只会让人更加的异想天开。多看看多走走，机会是自己争取的。

在未来找工作中，我觉得应该偏重人类必不可少的工作。所以说工作分365行，但实际上他们也有着本质上的区别。目前人类最低的保障是什么？那就是可以活着。如果连命都没了，那一切都是0。本次疫情中我看到了医生是必不可少的，粮食是必不可少的，通信是必不可少的，所以以后找工作尽量选择他们，因为在关键时刻他们可以保命。通信和网络则是让我们坐在家里，可以尽知天下事，从而为我们的未来做好充分的打算。

还有未来一定要有自己的车子，像房车那种可以做到，想走就走，想停就停。未来的时候我不知道会发生什么，但是我会做好充分的打算，准备应对未来的危机。话说靠天靠地不如靠自己，和平年代靠国家，动乱年代靠自己。在动乱年代不要去随意的挑战人性，就我自己来说，我写的这些也仅仅是为了保全自己而已。

在人类当中做好自己的角色，别人也应是如此。成长本来就需要一次次的突破，同时，在突破前要充分了解自己的情况，并默默的告诉自己：杀不死我的必将使我更强大。

关于河南高考的思考

都说河南高考难，可我觉得这并不有道理。河南本就是粮食大省，大部分我们身边的人都是农民出身。农民的子女为何这么努力去高考呢？无非就是羡慕城市的生活，挣钱改变家庭命运，所以他们都不愿意种地——太累太苦，生活还不好。如果大家都不想干这么苦这么累的活，那么其他人吃什么？当然这只是举个例子，国家肯定早就想到了，你们想考就考嘛，反正外省高校就那么多，这当然也就是理应当的。

说完为止，而剩下的工作或者复读这方面，国家可是把的死死的。我觉得这和之前出现的问题差不多，都是目光太短考虑不周导致的，那就是重男轻女导致的现在男的打光棍。如果当时有人反其道而行之，多生的闺女，那么光收彩礼就不知道有多少了。这些后果的原因，大多是一开始产生了偏见，仅考虑当下，目光不长远。

举个例子，如果我反其道而行之，学习向美国农民一样的经验，引进技术农具，然后找个较好的地方包几百亩地，再然后和食品公司签粮食协议，直接对口输送以防止中间商赚差价。虽然这只是想象，但对于目前情况来说，应坚持下地干活的大部分都是中年人和老年人。如果一直持续下去，地谁来种呢？没人种。不过话又说回来，这在我们合适不合适施行呢？谁知道呢？我也不知道。

良师与成长

果然在我们成长的时光里，遇到一个良师犹如千里马遇伯乐。成功讲的是天时地利人和，遇到良师，真的比自己单打独斗要好很多。

我之前曾经说过，社会是一层金字塔，由好到坏我们都很清楚。虽说塔顶难登，但塔顶大有人在；虽说塔底难活，但仍有人愿意前往。当我们长大成人的时候，我们就会选择在精彩中向上还是向下。有人会望光芒万丈，也有人会一身铁锈。

社会遵循自愿原则，因为大多数情况下拦不住的往往都是他们想拦住的人，赶不走的往往都是他们想赶走的人。塔顶虽然难登，但至少我们还应该登上去看看，体验一下高处不胜寒是什么感觉。为什么有人要往高处走？高处真的比低处要好。

为人处事的自律

最近又知道了许多事情。为人处事的道理中，我觉得我自己应该更适合其中的一种。我对自己的约束到目前来讲就是：在工作学习中可以畅所欲言，应讨论知识，需要大量的交流；其次，生活过程中除非遇到非常大的麻烦以及需要解决的事情，否则我绝不会多说半句闲话。

因为人人各异，我的想法观点，甚至幽默都不可能适合任何一个人。有时候，我随便说出口的一句话就可能引起不必要的麻烦，所以我何苦呢？并且在言语的过程中若提到精确的数字，则不可直接说出。世事无常理，继续努力吧。

梦与告别

之前听别人说，如果你梦见一个很久没有遇见过的人，那么说明他正在忘记你，所以他在梦里向你告别。我昨天就是这样，我说过我梦见过他一次，时隔许久，昨天晚上是第2次。不出所料，我仍然只是在远处看了几眼，即使在当时的情况下，我应该向他询问一些事情，可我并没有这样做。像平常一样我离开了。

在日常生活中都是这样，甚至我认为在他眼里我就是一个陌生人。我不知道这次梦见他是什么原因，即便是他忘了我也好，但是他的形象我会一直留着，像精神一样。

社会的规矩与层次

在不同的地方有不同的规矩，不同的人群，不同的强者。我们终究都会步入到社会中去，而社会的规矩，就是有能力的人去主导。有能力的领导人、有能力的领导社会、有能力的领导国家是不同的；人有能力帮助别人挣钱，有能力成为别人的下属也是不同的。

能力越大责任就越大，担负的越多。无论如何这都一定要做好。一个人不可能管得住14亿人，但他可以管得住各个省的管理人，不多也就二十几个人而已。各个省的管理人也不可能都管得住自己几千万的人口，但他只管理几个市的管理人就可以了。再往下是管县、县管镇、镇管村、村长管家长、家长管孩子，这样14亿人就被管住了。

这个社会就是强者胜任弱者退出的社会，所以有坏人是很正常的，有贪官才是正常的，有邪恶是正常的。既然他们形成了，那就说明社会有可以生成它的地方。为什么说这些是正常的呢？没有歧视哪有前进的动力？没有压迫，怎能推翻一个腐败政府？没有坏人怎能突出一个好人的好？

坏人不可怕，可怕的是装好人的坏人。钱是热水煮青蛙，后室温水煮青蛙是不一样的。什么样的政府就会有什么样的管理方式，但总体来说，只要是不影响人类发展的方式都可以，有点像“无过便是功”的意思。

管人先管心，可具体是什么样的方法才不会禁锢思想？我们是一个国家，你只要爱国就可以了，其次就是受思想的影响。如果生在农村还受腐朽的思想影响，那肯定是百害而无一利的；而如果出现在一线城市，常有外国人去旅游，那思想肯定又是一个档次。

自我创造与环境

无论何时何地，要想做好一件事情，首先给自己创造一个环境，其次是逼自己一把。而这些事情是对未来的一个准备，或者是干一件大事。想学习别人是被迫学习，但我可以主动学习。

自己所想的事情有的可以说出来，有的则不可以。感情是最容易打败一个人的利器。在各个行业有太多的问题需要我们去思考，如果我们不去经历，那么里面的某些事情可能我们永远不知道。

挑战自我是每个人成长的必需品，但也因其存在着危险系数，许多人都不愿自我突破。平凡的道路上总是人满为患，我就要与众不同。我们只有交过足够多的人，才能平静的看待这个世界。如我之前所说，什么样的环境造就什么样的人，我们所遇大部分人都来自各地，所以可以简单评估一下那个环境。

高考与国家发展

国家为我们提供了一个改变自己的机会，那就是高考。估计是国家缺科研人才，我们学习的全都是为了人类发展的知识。不管喜不喜欢，想要去好的大学就必须全都学会它们。

你是如何看待高考所大学的呢？去挣钱、去深造、去救国、去负荷？政府掌握着国家的一切，如果想要向政府挣些钱、权利、土地或者其他资源，那么就要为此付出相应的代价：时间、精力、健康甚至自己。每个人都这样，因此国家发展迅速。

其次我觉得思想的进步至始至终从未停止过。为了国家社会的繁荣稳定，绝不能有闲人；有闲人但绝不可有思想；有思想绝不可变坏思想。总之要斩掉一切的可能。还有一点国家政府就做得很好，那就是让更多的普通人和无业青年上岗就业。有人就会问那这不正合民意吗？是呀，让越来越多的人变成技术人员就业，有了收入，那么他们间接的不就是被管控住了吗？那些人会为了自己的工作自己的生活，而不会轻易的放弃这份养家糊口的机会，因此在他们忍耐范围内他们肯定会乖乖听话的。

也因如此我又想到一点，那就是未来机器人会替代普通技术工人的时代。我个人觉得应该不会是全部。按照目前的状态情况，机器人可以替代人工，但不能完全替代。首先应是从事于比较危险的工作、精密环境、少部分服务的环境、易偷工减量的环境等等。或许短时间内容易被机器人所替代。

为什么大部分人不敢呢？如果机器人真的把人替代了，大量技术人员失业，人人自危，这不刚好和上面的管理背道而驰嘛？这种情况并不是政府所看到的。但无论如何，人多放在哪里早晚都会出大大小小的问题。可是目前国家正在快速发展，这成果可都离不开我们这14亿人呢。

但我觉得不必担心，如果哪一天中国真的成为了超级强国的时候，就不需要那么多人了，毕竟资源是有限的。相比于10个人吃一口蛋糕和三个人吃一块蛋糕，差别还是很大的。所以到那时候，就会下令不要再生那么多孩子了，人够用就行，不是越多越好。

当然还是那句话，国务院的决定那绝对不会只看当下，至少适用未来很长的时间。因此我们根据国家的动向就基本知道目前我国所处的状态。就比如说今年或是这几年在教育方面，国家似乎需要更多的技术人才，这就是说明相比于目前国家还需要再奋战冲刺几年，又或许相比于思考的人多于动手的人，本科人才多于技术人才等等。也希望国家争取早日拿掉“发展中”的帽子，即便是少一些福利也罢。

心理与时间的苦恼

终于还是被动的提到了我的心理问题，这是我一直不敢说的。这几年我一直为精力的分配而感到苦恼，或许我也可以说“我应该把时间用在哪里？”

高三我用了几乎一半的时间去学习计算机方面的知识，收获也不小，知道一些网络方面的知识。而今年我又准备学习Linux系统方面的知识，但只学了一点，准备先停下来，全力投入学习中去。当然学习也是需要乐趣的，应该是英语吧，其次是数学。最近太累了，脑子转不快，也就想不了那么多了。

你的时间去哪了？我根本就没有时间概念。比我优秀的人都还比我努力，而我永远原地踏步。不是说我的成绩不好不如你们，我的态度、学习时间、努力程度都不如你们。我根本就没有时间去做其他的，在学习前的准备我一点都不重视。我的的确是该好好反省自己了，我需要改进的地方还有很多。

好坏兼并的现实

有太多太多的情况都是好坏兼并的：好事让人喜，坏事让人悲。在为自己当前所做事情感到高兴的时候，同时也应该为此带来的后果而令人担忧。我最不喜欢有这种事情的存在，可又不得不忍受。

无论是环境还是人与社会，都很难拥有两全其美的事情发生。即使环境允许了，那么外人也不会允许。无论自己做了什么，一旦放入群众当中，绝对有的人反对、有的人赞同、有人持中、有人弃权。所以还是先做好自己吧，因为无论自己做什么，绝对会有人反对的，所以还不如不让外人知道的最好。

其实吧，总的来说集体活动还算是一件好事，除了时不时的伤害了几个小个体的利益之外。任何时候都不会有公平这么一说，因为不是每个人都像孔子一样想的高看得远，也总有几个自以为是的人，搅了集体的和睦感。

首先我们应当面对的是：期望越高失望越大；社会是不公平的；每个人都不是完美的；自以为是的往往都是错误的；以及生活中的大部分负能量基本上都是差不多的，只不过是片面一些而已。

都说做人难，的确如此。我们永远琢磨不清自己和集体的关系，没有绝对的好与坏，不能只看别人的看法。对于自己来说很难做到对别人一律平等，因为人无完人。集体活动一定不能顺着别人的意见。

信息爆炸时代的思考

其实无论是谁，他所拥有的知识技能基本上都需要自己花时间与精力在上面。现在是爆炸性资源信息的世界，越来越多的信息导致我们很难有时间去深入的了解单条信息，而大多选择一扫而过。这就使得标题的作用、外表的装饰发挥到了极致，久而久之越吸引人的外观，往往获得更多的青睐。

我认为应当开发一款可以收集和整理信息的软件，这个好像收集搜索引擎一样，更应人性化一些。

年轻能力的浮躁规律

现在的大多数年轻人都可能会有这样的规律：能力和浮躁成正比。我觉得这种现象是不正常的，真正强大的人往往不会随意的表现自己。

言多必失，不知道有多少人说过，可又有太多的人栽在这上面。管好自己这张嘴可以写成一本书了，真是太重要了。生活中无处不在的微小行为都可能在挑战我们的能力极限。

提升能力的过程中，犹如往自己的桶里放各种物品。有些人放进一点再压压，一直稳固前行着；有些人装一个物品，那把桶晃的想掉出来两个物品一样。所以我们面对这些的态度，决定了我们未来的高度。

我又认为我在课程学习过程中需要一个可以管住我的人，督促我的学习，能够让我在其他地方飘完之后，可以快速冷静下来投入到学习中。以身作则，只是言辞一般都很难做到，所以我也需要被督促和监督，不能让我太自由的飘了。

对人性的观察

这次还是想说一下人性的问题。都说人性是可畏的，一般人不应该看透他，现在我倒挺感兴趣的。在我身边中也大概的有很多遇到过的人吧，我觉得有该交的也有不该交的。食物应该就是最简单的检测方法。

到目前为止有许多人，他们是我认为可以交往的，至少他们的行为我是认同的。在日常中不应该随随便便的帮助别人，应首先分析他们值不值得被帮。有些人帮助他们一次，他们感激不尽；有些人帮助如同填无底洞，永远填不完。这样倒不说，最终发现自己太傻。

因为每个人都有不同的想法，想法背后由思想高度支持。因此前面所说的后者当然不能说他们的行为是不可变的，也许未来就意识到了，但至少目前为止，他们这种便宜占尽却不想付出的人，应该不会有太多的人愿意和他们在一起的。

不要随便的生气，也同样要先分析自己值不值得生气。如果你走在大街上，一个地痞流氓看着你不顺眼，骂了你几句，怎么也要生个气给骂回去吗？虽说古人“以其人之道还治其人之身”，我觉得更应该适用于不是普通人，像你一做他们就知道是什么意思的人。

不要对牛弹琴，好的方法应应用于懂得这个方法的人。不要在俗人当中自演自命清高，再高有啥用？能当饭吃吗？除了被歧视还是被歧视。

生活与巧合的反思

其实生活就是这样，异性之间的巧合真的是太少了。当我们准备好表达自己的想法的时候，别人不是没有准备好，就是已经等的不耐烦了。都说人生没有彩排，每一天都是现场直播，我们又何苦不是这样的？我们所理想当然的一般结果十有八九都实现不了。

失望的原因要么就是怀有不该有的期待，要么就是痴心妄想。某些事情我们错过了，也许还可以重来，但大部分机会和人一旦错过了，可能就是一生的遗憾。所以我还是珍惜好身边的每一个人，不留遗憾。

在这里我想说一些，就是确实，我们和由于现在网络方面的原因吧，导致我们在日常生活中和别人打交道的机会越来越少，尤其是异性之间对吧？目的很明确，除非就是为了谈恋爱上床，然后怎么怎么样的。所以说就是也有很多一种陷阱产生的：就是你觉得很巧合，你三天两头碰见这个人，你觉得是因为巧合，但往往这其实背后可能是一场有组织有策略的安排，已经进了圈套里面了。

所以说这个社会很险恶，无论是电信诈骗还是骗人骗身，都还是需要注意一点吧。

放手和放弃是有区别的。

人生的意义与选择

无论遇到什么样的人，得出什么样的结论，都不要先说出来。有些人身在异地，心却远隔千里；有些人身在千里，心却心心相依。

人活一口气？为什么要这么说？我还是不明白我们生活在这个世界的意义是什么。无论是处在什么地位下，都有太多的被动和太少的主动。我们所做的一切事情说是为了自己，可最终还是别人的。房子再大顶多住了一生，车子再好也不过是坏了再修。平房也是住，别墅也是住；自行车也能跑，豪车也能跑——有什么区别呢？

有人冒险一生活在刀尖上，每天过得紧张的生活，他觉得这样的生活有意义；也有人平平淡淡一日三餐，结婚生子养老送终，他也觉得自己生活的很有意义。那到底是什么才有意义的生活呢？

Some of the things you think can be said, but some cannot. Emotions are the easiest weapon to defeat a person. In various industries, there are too many problems that we need to think about. If we don’t experience them, we may never know what’s going on.

Challenging yourself is a necessary part of everyone’s growth, but because it involves risks, many people are unwilling to push themselves. The ordinary path is always crowded, so I have to be different. We can only look at the world calmly after we have met enough people. As I said before, a person is a product of their environment. Most of the people we meet are from different places, so we can simply evaluate their environment.

The Gaokao and National Development

The country provides us with an opportunity to change ourselves, and that is the gaokao. The country probably lacks scientific research talent, as all the knowledge we learn is for the development of humanity. Whether we like it or not, we must master it all if we want to go to a good university.

How do you view universities from the gaokao? Are they for making money, for further studies, for saving the nation, or for burdening? The government controls everything in the country. If you want to get money, power, land, or other resources from the government, you must pay a corresponding price: time, energy, health, or even yourself. Everyone is like this, and that’s why the country is developing so fast.

Secondly, I think the progress of ideas has never stopped. For the prosperity and stability of the nation and society, there must be no idle people. If there are idle people, they must not have thoughts. If they have thoughts, they must not have bad thoughts. In short, all possibilities must be eliminated.

Another thing the government does well is getting more ordinary people and unemployed youth into jobs. Some may ask, “Isn’t this what the people want?” Yes, but as more and more people become skilled workers and get jobs with incomes, aren’t they indirectly being controlled? These people will not easily give up this opportunity to support their families and their lives, so they will obediently obey as long as their patience holds.

This also made me think that in the future, robots will replace ordinary skilled workers. I don’t think they will replace everyone. Based on the current situation, robots can replace manual labor, but not entirely. They should first be used in dangerous jobs, precise environments, some service jobs, and environments where there are opportunities to cut corners. Maybe they can be replaced by robots in a short time.

Why are most people afraid? If robots really replace people, a large number of skilled workers would become unemployed, and everyone would be in danger. Wouldn’t this go against the management I just mentioned? This situation is not what the government wants. But no matter what, having too many people will cause big and small problems sooner or later. But our country is developing rapidly, and this achievement is inseparable from our 1.4 billion people.

But I don’t think we need to worry. If one day China truly becomes a superpower, it won’t need so many people, because resources are limited. The difference between 10 people eating a bite of cake and three people eating a whole cake is huge. So at that time, they will order people not to have so many children, because having enough people is fine, and more is not necessarily better.

Of course, the State Council’s decisions will never just look at the present; they will apply for a long time in the future. So, we can basically know the current state of our country based on its direction. For example, this year or in the next few years, the country seems to need more technical talent, which indicates that compared to its current state, the country still needs to fight and sprint for a few more years. Or maybe there are more thinkers than doers, or more undergraduate-level talent than technical talent. I hope the country will strive to take off its “developing” hat soon, even if it means fewer benefits.

The Troubles of the Mind and Time

I’ve been forced to bring up my mental problems, something I’ve always been afraid to talk about. For the past few years, I’ve been troubled by how to allocate my energy. Maybe I should say, “Where should I spend my time?”

In my third year of high school, I spent almost half my time studying computer science, and I learned a lot about networking. This year, I was going to study Linux, but I only learned a little and decided to stop and focus on my studies. Of course, studying also needs to be fun, which should be English, and then math. I’ve been too tired recently, my mind is slow, so I can’t think too much.

Where has your time gone? I have no concept of time at all. People who are better than me are still working harder, while I am always standing still. I’m not saying my grades are bad, but my attitude, my study time, and my effort are not as good as yours. I simply don’t have time to do anything else, and I don’t pay attention to the preparation before studying. I really need to reflect on myself; there are still many areas I need to improve.

A Reality of Mixed Good and Bad

There are too many situations that are a mix of good and bad: good things make people happy, while bad things make people sad. When you’re happy with what you’re doing, you should also be worried about the consequences. I hate the existence of such things but have to endure them.

Whether it’s the environment, people, or society, it’s hard to have things that satisfy everyone. Even if the environment allows it, others won’t. No matter what you do, once it’s in the public eye, some people will oppose it, some will support it, some will be neutral, and some will abstain. So, it’s better to just do your own thing, because no matter what you do, someone will always oppose it. So it’s best not to let outsiders know.

Actually, all in all, group activities are a good thing, except for occasionally hurting the interests of a few individuals. There is no such thing as fairness at any time because not everyone thinks as highly and sees as far as Confucius. There are always a few self-righteous people who disrupt the harmony of the group.

First, we should face the fact that: the higher the expectations, the greater the disappointment; society is unfair; no one is perfect; the self-righteous are often wrong; and most of the negative energy in life is similar, just a bit one-sided.

They all say it’s hard to be a human, and it’s true. We can never figure out our relationship with the collective. There is no absolute good or bad, and we can’t just rely on others’ opinions. For oneself, it’s hard to treat everyone equally, because no one is perfect. In group activities, you must not simply follow others’ opinions.

Reflections on the Information Explosion

Anyone’s knowledge and skills are built on time and effort. We live in a world of explosive information, and the sheer volume of it makes it difficult for us to delve deep into a single piece of information. Most of the time, we just skim it. This makes headlines and attractive appearances extremely important, and over time, the more appealing the appearance, the more it is favored.

I think we should develop a software that can collect and organize information, like a search engine but more humane.

The Impatience of Young Talent

Most young people today seem to follow a rule: ability and impatience are directly proportional. I think this is an abnormal phenomenon. Truly powerful people do not show off easily.

The more you talk, the more you risk saying the wrong thing. I don’t know how many people have said this, yet so many fall into this trap. Controlling your mouth is so important that you could write a book about it. The small behaviors we do every day can challenge our limits.

The process of improving your abilities is like putting things into a bucket. Some people put in a little and pack it down, making steady progress. Others put in one thing and shake the bucket so much that it looks like two things might fall out. So, our attitude toward these things determines our future height.

I also think that in my studies, I need someone to keep me in check, to supervise my learning and help me calm down quickly after I’ve been distracted so I can get back to my studies. Words alone are often hard to follow, so I need to be supervised and monitored so I don’t get too carried away.

Observing Human Nature

I want to talk about human nature again. People say human nature is something to be feared, and that one shouldn’t try to see through it, but I find it quite interesting now. I have met many people around me. I think there are some I should get close to and some I shouldn’t. Food should be the simplest test.

So far, there are many people I think I can associate with, at least I agree with their behavior. In daily life, you shouldn’t help others without thinking. You should first analyze whether they are worth helping. Some people are grateful after you help them once. For others, helping them is like filling a bottomless pit; you can never fill it. What’s worse, you end up realizing how foolish you’ve been.

Everyone has different ideas, supported by different levels of thought. So, I can’t say that the behavior of the latter is unchangeable. Maybe one day in the future, they will realize it, but at least for now, those who only take advantage without giving anything in return will not have many people willing to be with them.

Don’t get angry easily. Analyze whether the situation is worth getting angry about. If you’re walking down the street and a hooligan curses at you, do you have to get angry and curse back? While the ancient saying, “to use a person’s own methods to deal with them,” might be suitable for people who are not ordinary—people who will know what you mean with just a hint—you should never play a lute to an ox. Good methods should be used on people who understand them. Don’t pretend to be noble among ordinary people. What’s the point? It will only lead to discrimination.

Reflections on Life and Coincidence

Life is just like this; coincidences between people of the opposite sex are truly rare. When we are ready to express our feelings, the other person is either not ready to listen or has waited so long that they are impatient. They say life has no dress rehearsals and that every day is live, so why should we be any different? The results we expect as a matter of course usually don’t happen nine times out of ten.

The reason for disappointment is either having unrealistic expectations or wishful thinking. While some things we miss can be relived, most opportunities and people, once missed, may lead to lifelong regret. So I should cherish every person around me to avoid regrets.

I want to add something here. Because of the internet, we have fewer and fewer opportunities to interact with others in daily life, especially with the opposite sex, right? The purpose is very clear: it’s for dating or other things. As a result, many traps are created. You might think it’s a coincidence that you run into someone every other day, but it may actually be a planned, organized setup, and you’ve already fallen into a trap.

So, this society is very treacherous. Whether it’s a telecom scam or a human-to-human scam, you still need to be careful.

There is a difference between letting go and giving up.

The Meaning of Life and Choice

No matter who you meet or what conclusion you draw, don’t say it out loud first. Some people are in the same place, but their hearts are miles away. Some people are miles apart, but their hearts are together.

Why do they say, “A person lives on a single breath?” I still don’t understand the meaning of our existence in this world. No matter what your status is, there is always more passivity than proactiveness. Everything we do, we say is for ourselves, but in the end, it’s for others. A house can be as big as you want, but you only live in it for one lifetime. A car can be as nice as you want, but you only fix it when it breaks. A bungalow is a home, and a villa is a home. A bicycle can run, and a luxury car can run. What’s the difference?

Some people live dangerously on the edge, living a tense life, and they feel that their life is meaningful. Others live a simple life, with three meals a day, marriage, children, and a funeral, and they also feel their life is meaningful. So what exactly is a meaningful life?

The answer is: Whoever feels their life is meaningful, that is the most meaningful life. No matter the system, environment, conditions, or standard of living—all the things you can judge—they lose to a single phrase: “I’m willing.” Yes, if everyone is “willing,” then what is fair or unfair? What is cruel or not cruel? What reason is there to change them?

The Control of Logic and Emotion

What good is it if we know all the so-called truths? Don’t we still have to do what we have to do? Don’t we still have to say what we have to say? I’ve been trying to regulate myself, but what are the results?

A very experienced homeroom teacher once concluded what the difference is between children from single-parent families and those from two-parent families. He concluded that children from single-parent families are more likely to have extreme behavior and impulses in the future, while children with normal parental care rarely show such behavior.

I don’t know why this problem exists, but I at least know that children from single-parent families are more likely to have issues. Recently, I’ve noticed that I rarely get angry, but when I do, I have strong impulses, something I don’t normally think about. So, I must control my emotions and not get angry. No anger is worth having.

While my rationality usually dominates, it can sometimes be affected by other thoughts. I’m most worried that one day evil will lead me astray. I need to find a way to figure out how to clarify the evil within me. No matter what I do, I must have a worst-case scenario plan.

I love rainy days, whether it’s cold or hot. As long as I can stay in one place quietly, I feel very relaxed and at ease.

Reflections on Suffering and Legacy

We struggle and fight in this chaotic world, enduring wind and rain, just to survive. What’s the point of surviving? To leave behind our descendants to continue to suffer, or to hope they will remember us?

Qin Shihuang was so great, but all we do is talk about him, then go on with our lives. People hope for peace and pave a path for their descendants so they can suffer less in this world. Is this possible? Maybe, at least for one generation. Like the children of today—who among the well-fed, well-dressed, and chubby ones didn’t have parents who supported them with their hard work and blood?

Theoretically, this can continue for a long time, as long as the parents are healthy and start early. This raises a question for me: will a child raised this way have the ability to support the next generation? It’s hard to say.

First, why did our parents’ generation work so hard to make money? Because they went hungry when they were young and often had no food or warm clothes. So, when they grew up, they had the motivation and a goal to fight for. But the recent generations of children have not had this experience. If they have not experienced pain, how can they grow strong in the future?

Sacrifice and Recognition

A person’s personal sacrifice and suffering can lead to temporary recognition and peace. I have been trying to figure out the relationship between human nature and people. My current method is to think from the perspective of a selfish person who doesn’t want to lose anything and only wants to get something from others. This description is not an exaggeration.

In my daily life, I’ve started to observe the next actions and behaviors of the people around me so I can categorize them. In a normal world, our elders could have easily summarized these things and published books, but why haven’t they? There’s a reason. It would only make the human heart more complex. But no matter what kind of spiritual wealth one has, they must first have material security.

The Weariness of Life

Right now, there’s only one word: tired. Life, studies, and social interactions—all these things are缠绕 around me. I just want to have a good sleep. Life is so long; there’s no need to overwork yourself. Being happy is one day, and being unhappy is one day. Many things are unnecessary. Live for yourself, live for yourself, live for yourself.

Real and Fake Emotions

Aside from the emotions within your own family or clan, most of the emotions you encounter in society are fake. But some people ask, “If that’s the case, why can’t I talk to my parents, but I have a great time with my friends?” This phenomenon seems to contradict what I just said.

Actually, the love from your family is the most genuine. Someone once explained this phenomenon, saying that a parent’s love is unconditional, while a friend’s love is conditional. Some also say that the reason we show a lack of love to our parents is precisely because we know their love for us is unconditional, so we often ignore and waste it. But friends are different. At the beginning, we don’t know each other well, so we don’t know if the other person is being sincere. Therefore, most people cherish the affection given by their friends.

There are no couples in this world who don’t argue. Many people yearn for the romance of a wedding, but I want to say that romance is temporary, and arguing is a lifelong thing. No good thing or matter in the world lasts forever. People grow old, but stories don’t. Although romance is only for a while, we shouldn’t miss it.

The biggest taboo when looking for a partner is judging by their looks. Appearance can be hidden, but personality can’t. The more easily something is hidden, the less it should be our standard for judgment. I’ve learned about this before but don’t remember the details now. I think some of the standards should be personality, hobbies, etc., which are essentially spiritual things that have been refined in the heart; everything else is unimportant.

Secondly, you should observe and learn about these things before you establish a relationship, to prevent cheating. After all this, I’d say the best relationship is simply when he makes a meal and calls you to eat, or when he gets paid and asks you to hold his money.

Life is like this: the greatest things are also the most ordinary; what is admired by all is also the most mundane. Some people say, “A relationship without the goal of marriage is just flirting,” but I want to say, “An argument without the goal of divorce is just a show of affection.”

Life is short, so you should enjoy it while you can. I don’t know how many times others have said this. You only truly understand why they say this after you’ve experienced it yourself. Strangers will always be strangers, especially those whose connections you can cut off at any time. We just need to listen to kind words and deflect the wicked ones; there’s no need to waste our time on these people.

We also need to understand: when you are strong, outsiders will depend on you; when you are weak, others will bully you. This phrase is a compass for us wandering in the ocean of life, so we don’t get lost. Remember, at any time, a sudden visit from a stranger or an unexpected act of kindness from someone you know should not be taken lightly. If they didn’t want something from you, they wouldn’t even come if you asked them to.

I often encounter these petty people. If you get fooled by them, the deeper you believe, the greater the harm you will suffer.

Kindness and Protection

We always want to face the world with a smile and show our kindness. But in the end, I find that the kinder and more friendly I am, the more others will see me as easy to bully and order me around. I know that the strangers on the street who make me angry are thugs, but I really can’t stand their attitude, and I have to stay away from them as soon as possible.

It seems that for anyone to show their beauty, they must first have the means to protect themselves. Only with this foundation can they better show their beauty. Of course, thinking deeper, is there absolute protection in this society? Maybe there are exceptions. I think most people get some minor injuries while being protected to avoid more major harm, or they trade their freedom for future stability.

This is the law of society: either someone is willing to take a loss, or everyone profits equally, or someone takes all the profit. If no one in this society was willing to take a loss, society would not be stable and there would be endless disputes. So, again, our happiness is built on our suffering, and “we” includes all living things on Earth.

With the current advanced information exchange, more and more scams are emerging, and they never care about the consequences for others. For adults, the impact may not be that big, but what about students? What about children? Although there are a lot of people exposing fraud now, the harm caused cannot be reduced or simplified in a short period of time.

There is no absolute good or bad; it is all relative. Many things are considered good because the majority says they are, so don’t be so quick to believe what others say. True friendship is seen in times of trouble; a person’s heart is seen in a major event. I think this is what human society is, and it’s not strange at all.

So, again, the world is vast, and there are all kinds of people. At least for us, when we see them, we don’t have to panic or be afraid. We should either stay away or act accordingly. The society I predicted earlier will likely come true, as long as the country can effectively control birth rates.

I think the reason there are so many people now is just to make them contribute to the development of humanity. After all, the world doesn’t need so many people, because resources are limited. When robot technology is perfected, a robot’s labor only needs to be charged and maintained, while a human worker would be much more troublesome.

I think the reason there are so many people left in the world is simply that the rulers in the middle don’t want to work. After all, labor is divided into big and small tasks, and someone has to do it. But if robots can perform most of the labor in the future, I think there will no longer be people in the lower class.

The Role of Women in Society

The role of women in human society is truly strange. The “hemline index” suggests that the shorter a woman’s skirt is, the higher the economic level of the society, and vice versa. I think I can also apply this internally.

In my school environment, the personality of girls also seems to reflect something. We all know that most men today lack masculinity, but at the same time, there are more and more “tomboy” types of girls. So, if a girl has a very strong personality, it means her father is likely very weak in her family, leading to a “strong woman, weak man” dynamic.

On the other hand, if a girl is very soft and has poor self-discipline, it may indicate that her family is very wealthy and she has received a lot of care and attention. The opposite is also true. This is where the saying, “Children of the poor learn to be independent early,” comes from. In short, the world is balanced.

The appearance of any phenomenon must have a reason, at least without human intervention.

The Social Rule of Resource Allocation

As I said before, the allocation of resources is a fascinating process. There are different levels, and resources are allocated from more to less. For example, two people, A and B, are a thousand miles apart. A wants to send a stick of butter to B but can’t do it directly, so A gives it to an intermediary, C. C takes it with his hand and passes it to D, who passes it to E, and so on until it reaches B. But because everyone in the middle touched some of the butter, only a small piece is left when it reaches B.

Everyone along the way has gained something. If the information isn’t passed along properly, B might receive the small piece and think A is stingy and not give anything back, and A might think B is greedy and stop sending butter. This is how conflicts arise.

So, to summarize this experience, if you want to be an ordinary person and live a stable life, then opportunities, hidden benefits, and good luck will likely never come your way. This is the law of society. As others say, “Opportunity favors the prepared mind,” and this is absolutely true.

Adaptation and Environmental Change

Everyone agrees that the more a person can adapt to society, the better they will live. But on the other hand, does this mean they are more easily changed from their original self by the environment? This is a terrifying thought because the success of these people depends entirely on their environment.

It’s like children can be divided into “stones” and “mud.” So, if a child is born into a terrible environment, having a “stone-like” personality is a form of self-protection to protect their heart and allow for better development in the future.

My recent “simplify the complicated” plan is to try to simplify complex things. I’ll write down what I need to remember in my daily life so that my limited brain energy can be used for more limitless creation. To put it more bluntly, the complex work I do now is to reduce my labor in the future. I’ll always have a notebook ready so I can write down ideas as soon as I have them.

There’s still a problem with the school’s management system.

Study-based Coercion and Impatience

Study-based coercion is a term I’ve recently coined, derived from “moral coercion.” It refers to a portion of students who use the name of studying to do things that have nothing to do with it. This can be summarized as a direct correlation between grades and arrogance: the better the grades, the less they are managed, and the better the grades, the more they can get away with breaking the rules.

Most people study to earn more money in the future, a small investment for a big profit. The original purpose of studying should be self-improvement, self-creation, and benefiting humanity, but now it’s all about making money, even if it’s not said out loud.

There are many smart and talented people, but not all environments allow them to succeed.
