第一章：国家国际

为公，国家壮大；为私，家庭壮大。

税收，人只有为自己努力的时候，才会全力以赴。

高考，每一个河南学子心中那个难以跨越的大山。

内卷，河南作为粮食和人口大省，内卷到最后，无非是粮食越来越便宜，学子越来越廉价。

答案，没有人愿意任劳任怨，可是社会的运转少不了任劳任怨。

游戏，作为游戏的策划者，制定游戏规则很有必要。

安慰，“正义也许会迟到，但不会缺席”。有时候，迟到的正义毫无意义。

我们都知道现在的社会是怎么样的，通过各种学校，将学生分成三六九等，通过分数来决定你能否接受优秀资源和好的学校以及工作。毕竟这些无论是物质还是精神的需求，都是属于国家所有的。我们普通人想要享受或者获得这些东西，就必须付出些什么，时间也好，精力也罢。它们只需要有人工作来维持这个社会的正常运转就行了。毕竟，在这个世界上，在许多普通岗位上，最终都会是有人来干的。我之前也刚刚总结出一句：他们所生活的幸福生活，都是建立在我们的苦难之上的。 当然这也包括我们自己，我们自己的话就是自给自足了。

现在的资本主义统治，就是更高级的奴隶统治。以前的统治阶级使用的是简单粗暴的手段，直接奴役人民。但随着时代的进步，人们都渐渐地有了思想，人们想要自由。可是统治阶级会这么简单地让你们自由吗？人民不为自己劳动，那还怎么活？

于是，他们制定规则，表面上让人们为了自己而努力，为了挣到钱，过上好日子，就不停地劳动。这可真是天大的笑话。反倒是，不知道的人一直劳动，知道的人就去割韭菜。当然，这可不能让所有人都知道“割韭菜”这种方式，那还得了。那就洗脑，思想的灌输。可不要说没有被洗脑过，我们的身边可是常常都有，什么“没有…就没有…”，“什么走过必须走的才能走想要走的”。“被卖了还在帮别人数钱”这些话一直都有。

那么会有人会反驳道，你说的难道不就是大家都认可的吗？有什么问题？其实我想说的就是：我们现在是知道了我们旧中国是一个封建社会，里面有很多的旧习俗，我们现在甚至还感慨为什么古人会做出那样的事情。可是如果真的把你放到那个时候，你指不定会做出一些令古人都意料之外的事情呢？

每一个时代都有每一个时代的局限性。如果你不是纵观全局的人，你永远也不会知道这个时代到底是怎么一回事。我也大多是猜测和推理。但是有一点肯定的是，如果我是一个领导者，我也会选择我所写的这些事。为什么？因为无论是谁做成一件大事，都不可能是完全按照道德的路线，或者是公平竞争的路线走出来的。

换句话说，水平差不多的人一起走同一条路，那么其中一个人凭什么能超过另一个人呢？这肯定不是耐力能够做到的，唯一能够说的就是他们的心思不同。有的可能就是只想平稳地、慢慢地走完这段路；有的可能想着如何超越别人，比别人走得更快更远；有的甚至想着如何绊倒别人。所以说放大了来看，人类的社会就是这样。所以说，从一个人所做的事情就能知道这个人心思有多么的复杂和缜密。尤其是做成大事的人，若没有超过常人的计划和心思，他怎么在能力都差不多的人群中成功呢？每一个成功都不是偶然的，但每一份努力都是必然的。 好了，今天就到这里吧。

人类的社会发展本就遵循着某些规律。其原因之一就是人们想要好好地生存发展，就必须前提有一个稳定的社会局面，用规则来约束普通人，然后在规则上进行工作。对于不遵守规则的人就应该受到惩罚。如此一来，坏人是什么？对于危害社会秩序，对他人生命构成威胁，让人感到恐惧的人称为坏人。对于一些不守规则而同样拿到了规则给的奖励的又算什么呢？

这个世界不需要所有人都努力工作，也不能没有人工作。所以只需要保证大部分人一直在底层的规则中维持基本运转，那么制定规则的人就可以用规则来毫不费力地拿到奖励。由于局限性以及圈子的封闭性，只要保证不让规则中的人知道，那么就可以为所欲为。

就像游戏中，本来需要每个人从0开始收集和积累金钱来实现升级，但总有人会使用外挂来快速达到高等级，这称为投机取巧。当然还有一部分人，就像游戏开发者，想要多少财富直接自己修改就可以了，只要没人知道，没人怀疑就可以了，这称为上帝模式。

Chapter 1: The Nation and the World

For the public, the nation thrives; for the private, the family thrives.

Taxes: People will only give their all when they are working for themselves.

Gaokao: That insurmountable mountain in the hearts of every student from Henan.

Involution: As a major province for agriculture and population, Henan’s involution ultimately leads to cheaper grain and cheaper students.

Answers: No one is willing to work without complaint, but the functioning of society depends on it.

Games: For a game designer, creating the rules is absolutely necessary.

Consolation: “Justice may be delayed, but it will not be absent.” Sometimes, delayed justice is meaningless.

We all know what society is like today. Through various schools, students are divided into different classes, and a test score determines whether you can access excellent resources, good schools, and jobs. After all, these things, whether material or spiritual, belong to the state. We, as ordinary people, have to give something—time or energy—to enjoy or obtain these things. The state only needs people to work to maintain the normal operation of this society. After all, in this world, there will always be someone to do the many ordinary jobs. I just recently concluded that the happy lives they live are built on our suffering. Of course, this also includes us; for ourselves, it is self-sufficiency.

Today’s capitalist rule is a more advanced form of slavery. In the past, the ruling class used simple, brutal methods to directly enslave people. But as times have progressed, people have gradually developed their own thoughts and desires for freedom. Will the ruling class simply give you freedom? If people don’t work for themselves, how can they survive?

So, they create rules. On the surface, they make people work hard for themselves, for money, and for a good life, so they labor endlessly. What a great joke. In reality, those who don’t know the truth keep working, while those who do know use the system to exploit them. Of course, you can’t let everyone know about this, right? That would be a disaster. So, they resort to brainwashing and indoctrination. Don’t say you’ve never been brainwashed; it happens around us all the time. Sayings like, “Without…, there is no…” or “You have to go through the necessary paths to go on the paths you want to.” Or even, “Being sold and helping the seller count the money.” These phrases have always existed.

Someone might retort, “Aren’t you just talking about things everyone agrees on? What’s the problem?” My point is, we know that old China was a feudal society with many old customs, and we wonder why our ancestors did those things. But if you were really put in that time, you might do things even the ancients would find surprising.

Every era has its limitations. If you’re not someone who can see the big picture, you’ll never truly understand what’s going on in that era. I, too, am mostly speculating and reasoning. But one thing is certain: if I were a leader, I would choose to do the things I’ve described. Why? Because no one who has achieved something great has done so by following a purely moral or fair path.

In other words, if people of similar ability are walking the same path, why should one person surpass another? This can’t just be about endurance. The only explanation is their mindset. Some may just want to walk the path steadily and slowly. Others may think about how to surpass others, to go faster and farther. Some may even think about how to trip others up. So, on a larger scale, this is how human society works. Therefore, from a person’s actions, you can see how complex and meticulous their mind is. How can someone who has achieved something great succeed among a crowd of equally capable people without a plan and a mindset that surpasses the ordinary? Every success is not a coincidence, but every effort is a certainty. That’s all for today.

The development of human society follows certain laws. One of the reasons is that if people want to survive and thrive, they must have a stable society. Rules are used to restrict ordinary people, who then work within those rules. Those who don’t follow the rules should be punished. So, what is a bad person? A bad person is someone who harms social order, threatens others’ lives, and causes fear. What about those who break the rules but still receive the rewards?

This world doesn’t need everyone to work hard, but it also can’t function without people working. So, as long as the majority of people at the bottom maintain basic operations within the rules, the rule-makers can effortlessly reap the rewards. Due to limitations and the closed nature of their circles, they can do as they please as long as the people in the system are unaware.

It’s like in a game, where everyone is supposed to start from scratch, collecting and accumulating money to level up. But some people use hacks to quickly reach a high level. This is called taking shortcuts. Then there are others, like the game developers themselves, who can change their wealth to whatever they want. As long as no one knows or suspects them, it’s called God mode.

第四章 人生感悟 观念与社会化的变迁因为人的观念不同，导致人与人之间彼此分开，也因为精神层次的不同，导致观念的相异。人与人之间应该是平等的，不能看的太高，也不能看的太低，因为每个人对别人的看法各异。无论是男生还是女生，都是从小长到大的，呼吸着同一片天空的空气，看着同一片蓝天，一起长大，一起为了自己的理想而奋斗，没有什么不同。可为什么一起到了一个新的场合之后，这一切都变了呢？虚荣心、攀比心、功利心，慢慢地建立了，这里越来越像社会了，越来越变得普通了。我曾亲眼见过一个人的变化，可我只能看，却无力干扰。 绝望与成长的选择应该有许多人会经历一个难熬的夜晚，绝望、无助、痛苦，充斥着大脑，很少有人能够帮助。可最终会发现，无论自己怎样的挣扎，也不过是在暴风雨下的一棵小草，世界才不会管我们这些，如同地球没了自己照样转一样。极端的人也许会自寻短见，也有人会在黎明后依旧微笑面对。这不是退缩，而是成长。世界的顶峰我们不可能永远占着，但至少我们也该到上面看看上面都有什么，哪怕只有一眼，至少我们曾经来过。 面对挑战与审慎处世世界一直在考验着人们。人对站在高处的态度心理，决定了他是否适合站在这个地方。失...
