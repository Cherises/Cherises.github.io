2022年6月23日

一、日记与感悟

文集忽然成了日记，记录着每天的生活总结与感悟。关于我的家事，没什么可说的，支离破碎。带来的结果就是家庭日渐衰落，一个人走了，一个人不回来，剩下的我只能独立去适应这个社会。现在的我变得心事重重，面对环境，我也非常敏感。写东西是我高中时期的常态，毕业后就很少有机会去写了。我现在更觉得，写这些东西是自我提升必不可少的一步。

二、资本与社会

现在的时代不同于过去，如今人手必备的手机和视频游戏正在侵蚀着我们的心态。对于我们成长的每一个人来说，都拥有独立自强人格的机会。但如今贫富差距如此之大。为何这个世界的运转少不了资本主义的推动，而资本主义的推动，必不可少的就是需要有源源不断的劳动力。廉价的劳动力也可以称之为**“割不完的韭菜”**。

可是话又说回来，没有哪一个聪明人愿意做那些底层的劳动，怎么办呢？利用人们各种各样的缺点，从而达到控制他们的目的。他们的目的其实彼此明确：让一部分人的子孙后代不断地努力工作，从而为其子孙后代去享福。其实，这也和旧中国的地主时代相差无几，只不过那时候太过刻薄，弄得老百姓饿得要命。现在其实也差不多，顶多算得上是升级版，让你们吃饱穿暖，能够让你们安安稳稳地过完一辈子。当然，我也向你们推送优质的产品和服务，把你们当作上帝，毕竟大多数人都不会满足于现状，想要升官发财。这不，一条上学路自费不说，到最后也只是培养了一帮打工人而已。

三、残酷现实

无论是刷视频还是看新闻，映入眼帘的，其中之一是男方考虑未来结婚的事情。我现在也不禁感叹。是啊，如今的选择选拔社会，像那些碌碌无为的年轻男性，面临单身一辈子的可能性。当然，我并不是担心他们，而是我自己和大多数人一样，无钱无势无背景，这未来可真是成了一个大问题。所以我需要不断地提高自己的认知水平和能力，闯出一番事业。

如今的社会现状就是那么的残酷与现实。有钱人是越来越方便了，因为许多人仅为了钱而为有钱人铺平了道路。他们是为了生活，是为了家庭和孩子，不惜拼命挣钱。女人在这个社会中被称为弱势群体，而在另一方面，她们也向着金钱与名誉靠拢，可以凭借自己的美貌，再加上一点魅力，就可以轻易被**“错作为胜利者”——也就是富家子弟选中。俗话说“女追男隔层纱”，但反过来却是“天壤之别”**。

在如今的社会之中，我所看到的是，一部分女人为了向财富靠拢，可以不惜出卖自己的身体。我曾经说过，对于社交，只有经历过足够多的人才能有显著的提升，看懂这是一个什么样的社会。算了，并不是所有的事情都可以总结。是啊，现在人们的观念不都是这样吗？没有钱，一切感情都无从说起。也许未来每个人都逃不开钱的命运，可是想成为金钱的奴隶还是其他的什么，都是自己选择。在网上我们能看到别人那些令人憧憬的故事，殊不知这些都是建立在所谓的**“资本”**之上。在未来的社会，你没有资本，就注定会被淘汰。

四、金钱与人性

现如今最明显的就是农村男性打光棍。整日被游戏动漫所精神围绕，无法自拔，不努力上进的后果就是如此。金钱可以帮我们做事更加顺利，但却不能成为金钱的工具。如果说别人无偿对我们的好，可以看作是一次投资，那实则真的是一点意思都没有了。通过这几年我的观察，也的确是验证了许多，名义如同一幅画。能用上的人未必显现它的价值，得不到他们的人，未必不能达到他的标准。小到家族辈分，大到国家官吏，无一不在运行这个规律。很多人往往用理由为自己的下一步铺平道路，并且也往往表面上看似帮助别人做好事。

在大国外交中曾有这样一句话：你实力弱小，别人打你都懒得找理由；你实力略次，你就是别人欺负的对象；你实力相当，别人就坐下来和你谈判；你实力过人，别人就拿流言蜚语诽谤你。 是啊，到头来这本身就是一个你争我赶，表面友好，背后激战的世界。只不过大部分人实力都差不多，另一方面谁都不想成为第一个动手的人。因此，在僵持的时间段里，我们大部分人过上了相对和平的年代。

五、人生准则与社会规则

回归正题，那就是做人的问题。我一直都是以低调为主。越是安静，听到的就越多。有的人穷极一生争取功名利禄，有的人恨不得让全世界忘了自己。中国的历史也算得上长久了，从历史中可以总结很多宝贵的经验，但历代都逃不掉历史的局限性。有一个疑问就是，史官为什么不能模仿？实则他们只会讲故事罢了。

2022年6月23日。人生压力、未来、梦想、发展、创业还是就业？随着我慢慢长大，越发地看清这个世界的本来面貌。在越发的利益驱使下，这个社会越发地没有人情味儿了。我的观点就是，在老一辈的时代，大家都没什么钱，只有患难见真情的感情，这种感情将伴随他们终身，即所谓的**“走亲戚”**来表现出来。

随着时代的发展，也就是我们这一辈的时候，同辈之间已不再有什么生死之交这一说了。更多的是从父辈那里获得的东西作为炫耀的资本，而对于父辈们而言，慢慢地专心给自己制造财富，也慢慢淡化了相比弱势的穷亲戚家的关系。

国家虽说是走的社会主义，可如今处处不离资本主义的缩影。一代又一代的，无不在追名逐利。在人人都只存私心的时代里，国家不会有公平，国家总是在受欺负的时代里异常强大，往往在和平年代里不攻自亡。我曾一度地相信**“天下大势，合久必分，分久必合”。而一切光辉的一面都是苦了老百姓。都说中国好，当我看到顶着“为人民服务”的名字，却做着伤天害理的事；当我看到拿着身份证在互联网上实名举报自己受到侵害的时候；当我看到扫黑除恶几年后，“为人民服务”的干部成了黑恶势力的保护伞的时候。也许这个国家早已不再是以前的那个国家了。只不过是一群打着正义的口号为自己疯狂敛财的人。正义慢慢地从缺席转为下岗**。

六、阶级、发展与制度

我想知道走向成功的人允许后人走他们的路吗？按照利益的角度，他们肯定不会。其实每一个家庭中年轻的孩子，只要没有什么太大的问题，都是有成才的机会的。只不过因为这个社会的各种不公平性，才导致了一部分人高举状元，一大部分人名落孙山。学习并不意味着只有在好大学才能取得成功。仅仅是一个环境和人脉。如今**“寒门难出贵子”，是因为他们在彼此环境下背负了太多的压力。一个出生贫困，整日为家庭琐事忙得不可开交的孩子，能和不愁吃不愁穿，一心只被父母专心学习的孩子能比吗？这是社会演变的产物**，我必须做出什么来改变我自己。

我现在的环境，我只能依靠我自己了，我必须变得强大，同时还要低调。一次就能治好的药不是好药，一吃就好的药才是好药。用利益去审视这个世界的时候，唯一不会错的是，它们是建立在底层劳动者的身上。我曾经称他们为寄生虫。换句话说，真正干实事的，并且只能重复地做一些事情的人，都是被寄生的底层劳动者。真正聪明的人是不干活的，挣死工资的人看似稳定，实则也是被寄生的人。但这些道理只有自己明白就好。如何让别人为自己打工才是最重要的。虽然免不了一些被别人寄生的苦，但这些都应该是为寄生别人而做的准备。这世界太过阴暗，只有真正有实力的人，他们的笑才最发自内心。

**“灯泡效应”使得几乎所有的企业照搬不放，只要钱来得快，没有所谓的道德底线，甚至法律的底线也时常越过。用劳动力换来的，终究挡不住别人智慧的转走，无能的人注定要被剥削。历史一直都在轮回。学的都是一样的东西，你如何能打败成百上千万的人。现在的学校和你想要参加工作之间的联系已经越来越远了。“学校跟不上社会”**早已成了主流。还是那句话，你所做的工作，只要有第二个人会做，你就会面临着失业的风险。

中国社会不同于国外，中国的人情社会是很难跨越的，阶级是几代人积累下来的。网上曾有人评论：“她给被人打下的努力，凭什么输给你十年寒窗事啊。”这个社会本来就是残酷的，各种**“潜规则”，各种“人情世故”，拆散了、打死了一代又一代年轻人的梦想。中国的制度决定了这一切，各种道德的约束底层劳动力者**。去国外发展是很有必要的。

七、生活的本质与选择

其实想一想这个世界的运行规律，都说历史是在轮回。爱情、亲情、友情，人活在这个繁杂的世界里，有些人无依无靠，孤苦伶仃，有些人生来富贵。生孩子，有的是用来继承家业，有的是则不明不白。有人说，人与人之间的差距，有时候比人和狗的差距都大。这似乎有些骂人的语气，但事实上，差距真的很大。可能是因为环境地域的局限性，人多的地方人来人往，自然学得精明，学习**“人情世故”、“明争暗斗”**。大到一个国家，小到一个家庭。

我时常在想，却大都是一些无用的东西。自从我高中毕业以后，就很少沉静下来，用心写些自己的所思所想了。而且我也慢慢地发现，现在我的精力、记忆也大不如以前了。不知是否是短视频看多了，好多也记不住，思想的反应也不如以往，莫非我已经老了？那不应该。我觉得是我纵欲过度导致身体过度劳累，也许是生活作息不规律，以上多种不良习惯致使如今的我的样子。我也知道历史有太多因为纵欲过度而致寿命缩减的案例，数之不尽，这可不行。我无非就是幼时缺乏管教，缺乏关爱，且没有什么不良习惯，使得我只有这一样放松方式，这样可行吗？谁知道呢？我呢，也只是记录一下，反思一下，至于能否解决，那不是我现在考虑的问题。

你说这个聪明的人都是怎么练成的呢？其实我记忆中的俩人都有一个共同点，那就是学习能力强，有野心、能干事、想干事、敢于追求自己喜欢的事情，因此他们成功的几率很高。而我恰恰相反，我是更倾向于呆在家里的胆小的人。其问题在于，我童年成长于溺爱的家庭中，导致我啥也不敢去做。而如今我也是思想成熟了，可行动上却止步不前。

情侣之间在一起最重要的是什么？其本质还是因为金钱。为何要这么说呢？无论是约会还是日常交流，都少不了要花钱。从我的朋友中所看到的皆是如此。女人交换的产品是价值，算了，现在说这些一点意义都没有，暑假这段时间却也稍放松了些，毕竟以前在学校很累，在家也能好好休息，松懈也无妨。

八、城市与未来

夏日固然行动方便，但也有天气炎热，蚊虫纷飞，但总是能够克服。傍晚时烧壶热水，冲泡茶叶，我甚是喜爱铁观音。树叶的清香是红茶的浓郁所不能比拟的。一杯热茶，一支笔，一本书，一本带血之本，而写之无尽。世俗繁华，是夜晚那灯火通明的小推车摊儿，是路边循环无尽的交通灯，是一排排商铺闪烁的霓虹灯，都在演绎着城市中的人间气息。

也就是说，未来是否会出现一种情况，那就是寒门难出贵子。现如今，先不说新生儿出生率是否降低，仅如今手机时代，有多少农村儿童因电子产品而沉浸在娱乐环境中无法自拔？我身边的例子比比皆是。小的时候天资聪慧，可是随着生活环境的影响，逐步沦为一个愚昧的普通人。尤其在农村这样的小环境，父母又是农民，整日精打细算、算计别人，最终接待接过其父母的衣钵，和他父母一样代代循环。但也真庆幸，人类在进化过程中只会遗留少部分基因，而并非生存知识，也杜绝了**“代代你家”**这种不切实际的行为。

从小了说，生儿育女只是防老。当然，对于不同的父母，其孩子自然扮演着不同的角色。富人是为了传承，穷人是为了欲望。而如今，这个世界自然还是有头有脑的人，聪明的人，他们自然不必担心传宗接代，这是顺理成章的事儿。

历来在中国，当有了国家之后，无论是封建王朝还是当下的社会主义、资本主义国家，于公于私都不可能让那些所谓的无所事事又贪贫困的人活下去。这不仅仅是动物世界的优胜劣汰，也是人类生存与进化所不能接受的。正是因为人们的欲望才能使进步与发展，无论是破坏损失还是成就，这些都是前进道路上所不可避免的。

I. Journal & Reflections

My collection of writings has suddenly become a journal, recording my daily life’s summaries and reflections. Regarding my family matters, there’s likely nothing to say; it’s fragmented. The result is a gradual decline of the family. One person left, another won’t return, and I am left alone to adapt to this society. Now I am preoccupied with worries and very sensitive to my surroundings. Writing was a regular habit for me in high school, but after graduation, I’ve had few opportunities to write. I now feel that writing these things is an indispensable step toward self-improvement.

II. Capitalism & Society

The current era is different from the past. The now-ubiquitous mobile phones and video games are eroding our mindset. Every one of us has a chance to develop an independent and self-reliant personality. Yet the gap between the rich and the poor is so vast today. Why is the world’s operation inseparable from the drive of capitalism, which in turn requires a continuous supply of labor? This cheap labor can also be called “unending chives to be harvested.”

But then again, no smart person is willing to do that kind of low-level work, so what can be done? They exploit people’s various weaknesses to control them. Their purpose is clear: to ensure that the descendants of some people work tirelessly, so the descendants of others can enjoy a life of leisure. In fact, this is not much different from the landlord era in old China, except that back then, they were so cruel that the common people were starving. It’s similar now, just an “upgraded version” that lets you eat and stay warm and live a stable life. Of course, they also offer you high-quality products and services, treating you like a god, since most people are not content with the status quo and desire to get promoted and rich. There’s a path for that: a self-funded education that ultimately only cultivates a generation of workers.

You worked hard and became a useful tool for others. Of course, you also received a reward. It seems like you’ve earned a lot, but you are still a worker.

III. A Harsh Reality

Whether I’m scrolling through videos or reading the news, one of the things that catches my eye is men thinking about marriage. I can’t help but sigh. Yes, in today’s selection-based society, young men who have achieved little are facing the possibility of staying single for life. Of course, I’m not worried about them, but about myself. Like most people, I have no money, no power, and no background, and this has become a huge problem for my future. So I need to continuously improve my cognitive abilities and skills to carve out a career.

The current state of society is so harsh and realistic. It’s getting more convenient for the wealthy, because many people pave the way for them just for money. They work hard for their lives, their families, and their children. Women are called a disadvantaged group in this society, but at the same time, they are moving toward money and fame. With their beauty and a bit of charm, they can easily be “mistaken for a winner”—chosen by a wealthy heir. As the saying goes, “it’s easy for a woman to pursue a man,” but the reverse is a “world of difference.”

In today’s society, I see some women who are willing to sell their bodies to get closer to wealth. I once said that when it comes to social skills, you can only significantly improve and understand what kind of society this is by interacting with enough people. But then again, not everything can be summed up. Yes, isn’t this what people think now? Without money, no emotion can even begin to exist. Maybe everyone’s fate is tied to money in the future, but whether you become a slave to money or something else is your choice. Online, we see people’s beautiful stories, but we don’t realize these are built on so-called “capital.” In the future, if you don’t have capital, you are destined to be eliminated.

IV. Money & Human Nature

The most obvious phenomenon now is that rural men are unable to find wives. Being lost in games and anime all day, they can’t get out of it, and this is the consequence of not working hard. Money can make things go more smoothly, but you cannot be a tool for money. If someone’s unconditional kindness to us can be seen as an investment, then it really loses its meaning. From my observations over the years, I’ve confirmed that reputation is like a painting. Those who have it may not realize its value, and those who don’t may not fail to meet its standards. From a small family hierarchy to a large national bureaucracy, this rule is at play everywhere. Many people use excuses to pave the way for their next move and often seem to be helping others on the surface.

There’s a saying in international diplomacy: “If you are weak, others won’t even bother to find an excuse to hit you; if you are slightly weaker, you will be bullied; if you are an equal, they will sit down and negotiate with you; and if you are too strong, they will slander you with rumors.” Yes, in the end, this is a world of competition, with a friendly surface but a fierce battle underneath. The thing is, most people have similar strength, and no one wants to be the first to make a move. Therefore, in this stalemate, most of us have lived in a relatively peaceful era.

V. Life Principles & Social Rules

Back to the main topic: how to be a person. I have always focused on being low-key. The quieter you are, the more you hear. Some people spend their whole lives pursuing fame and fortune, while others wish the whole world would forget them. China’s history is long, and we can learn a lot from it, but every dynasty has failed to escape the limitations of its time. I have a question: why can’t historians be copied? In fact, they only tell stories.

June 23, 2022. Life pressure, future, dreams, development—should I start a business or find a job? As I grow up, I am seeing the true face of this world. Driven by ever-increasing profit, society is becoming more inhumane. My view is that in the era of my elders, everyone had little money, and relationships were based on true feelings revealed in times of trouble. This kind of affection will accompany them for life, as shown in the so-called “visiting relatives.”

With the development of society, in our generation, there is no such thing as lifelong friendship anymore. What’s more common is using things obtained from our parents as a means of showing off. As for our parents’ generation, they slowly became focused on making their own wealth and gradually faded out of their relationships with their disadvantaged relatives.

The country claims to be socialist, but it is full of capitalist shadows. Generation after generation, everyone is chasing fame and profit. In an era where everyone is selfish, the country won’t be fair. A country is always exceptionally strong when it is being bullied, and it often self-destructs in peacetime. I once believed in the ancient saying, “The world situation, long divided, must unite; long united, must divide.” And all the glorious parts of it are built on the suffering of the people. They all say China is good, but when I see people who claim to “serve the people” doing terrible things; when I see people holding their ID cards and reporting online that their rights have been violated; and when I see that after a few years of fighting against organized crime, the “civil servants” who claim to “serve the people” have become the umbrellas for that organized crime. Maybe this country is no longer the same country as before. It’s just a group of people who are using a slogan of justice to amass wealth for themselves. Justice is slowly transitioning from being absent to being laid off.

VI. Class, Development & Systems

I wonder if people who have achieved success allow others to follow their path. From the perspective of self-interest, they certainly would not. In fact, any young person from any family, as long as they don’t have major problems, has a chance to succeed. But the various unfairness of this society leads to a few people becoming top students while the majority fails. Learning doesn’t mean you can only succeed at a good university. It’s simply about the environment and connections. Now, “it’s hard for a poor family to produce a great child” because they have too much pressure in their environment. Can a child born into poverty, who is busy with family chores all day, be compared to a child who doesn’t have to worry about food and clothing and can focus only on their studies? This is a product of social evolution, and I must do something to change myself.

In my current environment, I can only rely on myself. I must become strong and at the same time, be low-key. A medicine that cures you with one dose is not a good medicine; a medicine that works once but is needed again is a good medicine. When you look at the world through the lens of self-interest, the one thing that is always true is that they are built on the backs of low-level workers. I once called them parasites. In other words, those who do the real work and can only do repetitive tasks are the low-level workers who are being parasitized. Truly smart people don’t do the work. People who earn a fixed salary seem stable but are also being parasitized. But these are truths that you only need to understand yourself. The most important thing is to know how to get others to work for you. Although you will inevitably be parasitized by others, these hardships should be a preparation for parasitizing others yourself. This world is too dark; only those with true power have a genuine smile.

The “light bulb effect” has been copied by almost all companies. As long as money comes quickly, there’s no so-called moral bottom line, and even the legal bottom line is often crossed. What is earned through hard labor can never stop being transferred away by others’ cleverness. The incompetent are destined to be exploited. History keeps repeating itself. If you all learn the same things, how can you defeat millions of others? The connection between schools and the job market is becoming more and more distant. “Schools can’t keep up with society” has become a mainstream idea. Again, if there is a second person who can do your job, you face the risk of losing it.

Chinese society is different from foreign countries; the human network system is difficult to overcome here, and class is accumulated over generations. Someone once commented online, “Why should her years of hard work be defeated by your ten years of studying?” This society is inherently cruel, with various “unwritten rules” and “social niceties” that have shattered and killed the dreams of generations of young people. China’s system determines all of this, with various moral constraints on low-level workers. It is necessary to go abroad to develop.

VII. The Essence & Choices of Life

In fact, when you think about how the world works, they all say history repeats itself. Love, family, and friendship—people live in this complex world. Some are isolated and lonely, while others are born rich. Some people have children to inherit a family business, and others have them for no clear reason. Someone said that the gap between people can sometimes be bigger than the gap between people and dogs. This sounds a bit like an insult, but in fact, the gap is really huge. Perhaps due to the limitations of the environment, where there are many people, they naturally learn to be shrewd, learning “social etiquette,” and engaging in “open and secret struggles.” This applies to a country as a whole, down to a small family.

I often think about these things, but they are mostly useless. Since I graduated from high school, I have rarely settled down and seriously written down my thoughts and feelings. I have also gradually found that my energy and memory are not as good as they used to be. I wonder if it’s because I’ve been watching too many short videos; I can’t remember many things, and my mind is not as sharp as before. Am I getting old? It shouldn’t be. I think it’s because I’ve been overly indulgent, leading to physical exhaustion, and maybe an irregular lifestyle. These bad habits have led to my current state. I also know that there are countless historical examples of people whose lives were shortened due to overindulgence. This can’t continue. I think my situation is due to a lack of discipline and care in my childhood and a lack of bad habits, so this is my only way to relax. Is this okay? Who knows? I’m just recording and reflecting. As for whether I can solve it, that’s not for me to consider right now.

How do you think smart people become who they are? The two people I remember have one thing in common: they are quick learners, ambitious, capable, willing to work, and brave enough to pursue what they love. Therefore, their chances of success are high. I am the opposite; I am a timid person who prefers to stay at home. The problem is that I grew up in an overly-doting family, which made me afraid to do anything. Now, my mind is mature, but my actions stand still.

What is the most important thing in a relationship? At its core, it’s about money. Why do I say this? Whether it’s for dates or daily communication, money is always needed. This is what I’ve seen in all my friends’ relationships. Women exchange for value. Anyway, there’s no point in talking about this now. I’ve been a bit more relaxed this summer. After all, I was very tired at school, and it’s okay to take it easy and rest at home.

VIII. City Life & The Future

Summer is certainly a good time to be active, but it’s also hot and buggy, though you can always overcome these things. In the evening, I boil water to make tea. I love Tieguanyin. The fresh aroma of the leaves is incomparable to the richness of black tea. A cup of hot tea, a pen, a book, a notebook of blood, and endless writings. The worldly hustle and bustle is the brightly lit food stalls at night, the endlessly cycling traffic lights on the side of the road, and the flashing neon lights of the shops. They all portray the human atmosphere of the city.

This brings me to the question of whether a situation will arise where it’s hard for a great child to emerge from a poor family. Now, not to mention whether the birth rate is dropping, but in the age of smartphones, how many rural children are immersed in entertainment and unable to escape? I see examples of this everywhere. When they were young, they were naturally intelligent, but because of their living environment, they gradually became ignorant ordinary people. Especially in a small rural environment where parents are farmers, constantly calculating and scheming against others, the children eventually take over their parents’ legacy and continue the cycle. But I’m glad that in the process of human evolution, only a small portion of genes, not survival knowledge, is passed down, which prevents this unrealistic “generation after generation” behavior.

Simply put, having children is for old age support. Of course, for different parents, their children play different roles. The rich have children for inheritance, and the poor have them for desire. In this world, there are people with brains, and smart people naturally don’t have to worry about carrying on the family line; it’s a matter of course for them.

In China, since the establishment of a country, be it a feudal dynasty or today’s socialist and capitalist nations, it’s impossible, both for public and private reasons, to allow those who are idle, greedy, and poor to survive. This is not only the survival of the fittest in the animal kingdom, but also something that human survival and evolution cannot accept. It is people’s desires that lead to progress and development. Whether it’s destruction, loss, or achievement, these are all inevitable on the road forward.
